Get to know us

Lwazi Casting Agency is working towards alleviating the level of unemployment, crime, poverty and involving the youth in our company, more especially in the rural areas and townships since there are no community projects to raise awareness on such issues.
Unity Media House and Lwazi Casting Agency have new faces that have never been used before. We train or artists by undergoing acting workshops. We also have a good relationship with some of the industry actors/actresses and artists that train our crew in the workshops.  We constantly do motivational road shows targeting potential talents. We motivate the youth to partake in careers such as music, modelling, dancing, acting or script writing.  
Unity Media House and Lwazi casting agency strong believe that the youth has a lot of potential and so much to offer that is yet to be discovered and unacknowledged.The company is working towards divulging the raw talents and skills so as to turn them into greatness.

                                                                        AIM

We are aiming to work and uplift different artists and talents such as actors, actresses, poets, scriptwriters, models, dancers, musicians and film production. We make the unknown visible to the world.

                                                                        VISION

Our vision is to become a great international production company. We strive to develop the community by creating opportunities to the youth. We train disciplined, courteous, professional and reliable artists.

                                                                        MISSION

We create the opportunity of becoming a great artist in order to uplift the community you come from. The trainings provided expose the raw talents within the communities and provide them with necessary support as well as innovatively mobilize the community's resources by providing education, sports fields and empowering current and future entrepreneurs within the community.

                                                                     OBJECTIVES

*To provide community service.

*To work in collaboration with our companies and organizations regarding the community's transformation and edcuation.

*To explore methods of capacitating in other fields than in their original expertise.
